---
name: Language convention
description: Project files in English. Technical terms preserved in their original language when there's no natural translation.
type: feedback
---

All files, folder names, code, comments, and written content should be in English.
Technical terms without a natural translation stay in their original language (e.g., skill, Claude Code, CLAUDE.md).

**Why:** The target audience is English-speaking developers. Clarity for the people who actually read it matters more than theoretical global reach.

**How to apply:** Every file created or edited should be in English. Established technical terms may remain in their original language.
